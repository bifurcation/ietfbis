Re-booting the IETF

The IETF is the standards body for the Internet. We maintain archives of documents about the Internet, especially specifications of protocols and data formats that enable interoperability -- the "Inter" in "Internet".

This repository is a test-bed for developing ideas related to a clean-slate redesign of the IETF's processes.
